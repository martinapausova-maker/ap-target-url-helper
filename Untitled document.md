"AP" stands for *Anchor Planning* and refers to the records found in the **AP** tab of SeaTable. Each AP record includes key details such as the **anchor text** and the corresponding **target URL**.

Selecting the appropriate AP for a given site is a critical part of our outreach process. The chosen anchor and URL must be a natural fit for the site and its content. To ensure alignment, we aim to have the team follow the below criteria:

1. The AP must be contextually appropriate for the site’s theme and audience.  
2. The page being linked to (Target URL) should logically fit within the content and overall site structure.  
3. There should be relevant or supporting content on the site.

For **FanDuel-related** APs specifically, additional considerations include:

1. Whether the sport is currently in season.  
2. The priority level of the sport at the time.  
3. The presence of similar or complementary content about the sport.  
4. Overall site relevance to sports and FanDuel's audience.  
5. Reference to the [**Sport Calendar**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1YNCX9GcAVIc3DxIEPNcCTwEmugoMpG0pLHznLY-_ML4/edit?gid=0#gid=0) to inform timely AP selection.

To assist with this process, we have developed decision trees for select clients. These resources are designed to guide the selection of the most appropriate AP for each placement:
